What is My Calcium?

My Calcium is a free, privacy-focused Progressive Web App designed to help you monitor your daily calcium intake. Whether you're managing osteoporosis, supporting bone health during pregnancy, or simply want to ensure you're getting enough calcium, this app makes tracking simple and effective. With a comprehensive food database, barcode scanning, and insightful statistics, you'll have everything you need to maintain healthy calcium levels.

Privacy First. Always.

Your health data is yours alone. My Calcium stores all data locally on your device with no accounts required. The optional sync feature uses end-to-end encryption, ensuring that even the server cannot access your information. No analytics. No tracking. No third parties. Just you and your data.
