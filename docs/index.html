Everything You Need

Comprehensive Database

Quickly search thousands of food items from the USDA food database to log your entries.

Cross-Device Sync

Use the app on your phone and tablet with seamless, end-to-end encrypted data synchronization.

Custom Foods & Favorites

Add your own custom foods and mark any item as a favorite for even faster tracking.

Insightful Statistics

Visualize your intake with daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly charts to see your progress at a glance.

Works Offline

As a full Progressive Web App, My Calcium works perfectly even without an internet connection.

Data Export & Reports

Generate printable reports to share with a healthcare provider and create full backups of your data.

Privacy First. Always.

Your health data is yours. My Calcium is a "local-first" app, meaning all data is stored directly on your device. The optional sync feature uses end-to-end encryption, so not even the server can read your data. No analytics, no tracking, no accounts needed.

Open & Transparent

My Calcium is free and open-source software built with modern, privacy-respecting technology.
